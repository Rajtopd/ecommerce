Our Story

Where Culture
Meets Couture

Soul Sisters was born from a deep love of Indian craft and the desire to carry it gracefully into modern life. Based in Dubai, we curate ethnic wear that honours centuries-old weaving traditions while fitting seamlessly into a contemporary wardrobe.

Each piece is thoughtfully sourced from artisan clusters across India — from the silk weavers of Kanchipuram to the block printers of Jaipur.
